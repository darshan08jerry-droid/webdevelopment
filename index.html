About the Studio

Design is a tool,
not a decoration

Pixel & Craft was born out of a simple belief: great design should be accessible to every business, not just the ones with enterprise budgets. We work closely with founders and small teams to bring clarity and beauty to their brand.

Our studio is small by choice. That means direct collaboration, faster decisions, and work that truly reflects who you are — no generic templates, no junior handoffs, no fluff.

How We Work

Four steps to a brand
you'll be proud of

01
Discovery

We deep-dive into your business, your audience, and your goals. No assumptions — just listening and asking the right questions.

02
Strategy

We map out a creative direction, define visual references, and align on what success looks and feels like for your brand.

03
Design

We craft the visual identity — logos, palettes, type, layouts — iterating with your feedback at each stage of the process.

04
Delivery

You receive production-ready files, a brand guide, and a walkthrough — everything you need to use your new brand confidently.
